Full Start-to-End Guide to Upload .AAB to Play Store STEP 1 — Create Google Play Console Account • Go to Google Play Console • Sign in using your Google account • Click Create Developer Account • Pay $25 (one-time fee) • Fill Developer name, Email, Phone number, Address • Verify your identity (OTP/email) • After this, your developer account is ready. STEP 2 — Create New App • Click Create App • Fill App Name, Default Language, App Type, Free or Paid • Tick declarations • Click Create App • Your app dashboard opens. STEP 3 — Upload .AAB File • Go to Dashboard  →  Release  →  Production  →  Create New Release • Click Upload • Select your .AAB file • Wait for processing • Add release notes • Click Save STEP 4 — Upload App Assets • Upload App Icon (512×512 PNG) • Upload Banner (1024×500 PNG/JPG) • Upload Screenshots (Minimum 2, Recommended 4–6) • Screenshot size: 1080×1920 (9:16) • Click Save STEP 5 — Write App Description 
• Add Short Description (80 characters max) • Add Long Description (up to 4000 characters) • Click Save STEP 6 — Add Privacy Policy • Go to Policy  →  App Content  →  Privacy Policy • Add Privacy Policy URL • Required if app uses login, internet, or stores data STEP 7 — Fill App Content Details • Fill Target Audience • Fill Data Safety • Fill Ads Declaration • Fill Content Rating • Fill App Category STEP 8 — Content Rating • Go to Policy  →  Content Rating • Answer questionnaire • Submit to receive rating STEP 9 — Provide Demo Login (If Needed) • Go to Policy  →  App Access • Provide demo email and password if login required STEP 10 — Select Countries • Go to Release  →  Production • Select countries (e.g., India) • Click Next STEP 11 — Submit for Review • Go to Production  →  Review Release • Click Start Rollout to Production • Submit for review STEP 12 — Wait for Approval 
• First-time review takes 1–3 days • Updates usually take a few hours to 1 day • After approval, your app goes live
